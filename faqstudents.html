Students’ Frequently Asked Questions
Who is eligible to apply for 2hats?
All students are able to apply to 2hats. We are open to students from all backgrounds and faculties.

Am I eligible to apply as international student?
Yep! However, there may be restrictions on your working hours as required by Australian Law.

Can I get a work opportunity overseas or in another state through 2hats?
Unfortunately, not at the moment. However, we are expanding our services to other states within Australia and internationally very soon.

Which industries specifically am I able to apply for?
Whilst you have the option of choosing preferred industries that you would like to be placed into, you may receive an offer from 2hats from a similar industry based upon your skills and the information we receive during the selection process.

What specifically did you require to get a placement through 2hats?
Whilst other systems may be looking for just academic excellence, we at 2hats are trying to find students that are the best fit for the role! We help make sure that you have the best chance of receiving a placement through our feedback process and have multiple avenues of support throughout.
Can I Apply?
Are the professional placements paid?
Yep! You will be paid on a contract basis. After the placement, depending on your performance and the resources of the company, you may receive an offer of ongoing employment that is determined between you and the company. The amount that you will receive will depend on the company and the role.

Do I need to pay for the service?
Nope! Our core career development service is completely free for students. We don't charge students thousands of dollars to receive a professional placement.

Why should I go through 2hats, instead of any other job portals?
2hats isn't a job portal or a recruiter. As a career development service built for students we help improve and grow you, placing you into paid placements and kickstarting your career with relevant experience.

What's the benefit of me going through the assessment with 2hats?
2hats connects with paid professional work opportunities and upskills you through the process. Unlike our competitors, we help improve your value with feedback and training as you go through our selection process.

What kind of companies 2hats are placing students to?
2hats has a broad range of company placements from a variety of industries.

Is my personal information safe?
Your privacy is incredibly important to us and we have a strong commitment to keep your information private. Your personal information will not be disclosed to any third party unless you give us permission. For example, it will be used to review your resume, or to contact the companies about the placement.


Do I need to pay? Or do I get paid?
How can I join the platform?
Register your free account on our website home page and upload your resume. The app is available on the Apple App Store and the Google Play Store. Just search for '2hats'.

I can't find your app in Google Play or Apple App Store!
Unfortunately, we are only available in Australia at the moment! If you are an overseas student please make sure to switch your app store to the Australian market.


I'm in! How can I register?
Is there any difference between submitting my profile through App or Web?
Essentially both the 2hats App and Web Portal are the same. You are able to submit your profile and resume through both.

What are the processes involved to get a placement?
The very first step is to make sure that you join our community by submitting your resume and getting feedback from the 2hats team.

How does the feedback system work? What should I do after receiving a feedback?
We want to help you grow, which is feedback is at the core of 2hats. Once you submit your resume you'll either get a notification letting you know you have passed to the next round or which areas need improvement. You are able to resubmit up to 3 times with multiple feedback documents from the 2hats team.

Should I fill in the profile or upload the resume?
You have the option of going with either. As long as you fill in your profile or upload your resume, you'll be added into review by the 2hats team.

How long do I need to wait for my feedback?
This varies as some candidates will progress to the next stage where as others may receive the feedback to see where they need to improve. You'll receive this feedback as soon as possible.

Will I be interviewed by the company again?
Once you have completed your placement you won't be interviewed again. The 2hats team will use your original responses in our system as well as the feedback from your past working histories to place you into another company.

How long is the professional placement?
It varies depending on the structure and the arrangement, in general, the initial contract is between 3-6-month, subject to negotiation between you and the company. It will all be clearly outlined before you begin.
