Our Mission

Our Story

What you do, it’s more than just work or a job. Our entire team itself has been built from this vision. It’s a part of who we are, we are what we do. Finding that true pathway however, requires experiences that many young people aren’t able to find.

“We wanted to create something more accessible, that helped people get to where they needed to be” — Gloria Li, Co-Founder

That’s why we built 2hats. We want to give every student the opportunity to achieve their full potential, to discover their purpose and where they can have the greatest impact. Finding true passion in the work you do - that’s our ultimate goal.
